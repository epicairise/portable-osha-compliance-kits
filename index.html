Frequently Asked Questions

What should a portable OSHA compliance kit include?

A solid kit includes PPE, hazard signage, first aid, documentation logs, and OSHA-required postings — all in a mobile, durable container.

Are these kits OSHA certified?

OSHA doesn’t 'certify' kits, but these kits are designed to meet OSHA standards for various industries and come pre-loaded with compliant materials.

Can one kit cover multiple job types or trades?

Yes — many kits offer modular or customizable options to fit general, construction, electrical, or industrial OSHA requirements.
